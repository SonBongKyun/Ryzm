Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ryzm pricing

Can I use Ryzm for free?

Yes! The Free plan gives you access to core features including AI Council (10/day), Trade Validator (3/day), basic panels, and TradingView charts. No credit card required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe for secure payment processing. All major credit cards, debit cards, and select local payment methods are supported.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel your subscription at any time from your profile. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a yearly discount?

Yes! Choose the yearly plan and save 17% — just $200/year instead of $240. That's $16.67/month for full Pro access.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is preserved. You'll just lose access to Pro-only panels and features, but all journal entries and history remain available within Free limits.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. We offer a full 7-day money-back guarantee on all Pro subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, just reach out and we'll process your refund — no questions asked.
